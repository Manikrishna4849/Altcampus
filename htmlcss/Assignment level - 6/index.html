Lord Rama

The Ram Mandir, also known as the Ram Janmabhoomi Temple, is a Hindu temple that is being constructed in Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh, India.

It is dedicated to Lord Rama, a significant figure in Hinduism, and is believed to be the birthplace of Lord Rama. The temple has been a subject of controversy and legal disputes for several decades.

The dispute revolved around the Babri Masjid, a mosque that stood at the site where Hindus believe Lord Rama was born. In 1992, the Babri Masjid was demolished by a group of Hindu activists, leading to widespread communal tensions. The legal battle over the ownership of the site continued for years.
