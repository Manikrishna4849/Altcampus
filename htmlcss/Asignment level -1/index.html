The construction of the Ram Mandir in Ayodhya stands as a historic and symbolic milestone in the cultural and religious landscape of India. This sacred temple is dedicated to Lord Ram, a revered figure in Hinduism, and its inception marks the end of a long-standing legal and socio-political dispute over the site. The foundation stone was laid in August 2020, embodying the aspirations and sentiments of millions of devotees who consider Ayodhya as the birthplace of Lord Ram.
